Good to know

Questions,
answered.

Is my journal data secure?＋

Yes. Your journal is protected with device authentication, encrypted local storage, and password-encrypted backup options.

Can I export my records?＋

Yes. You can create professional PDF journal records and optionally append supporting image and scanned-document attachments.

Does it support iCloud backup?＋

Yes. Automatic backups can run after journal changes and when the app enters the background. Backups are encrypted using your password.

Can I customize it for my work?＋

Yes. Add your own document types, identification methods, checklist items, and professional profile details.

Is NotaryJotter legal in my state?＋

NotaryJotter was built with Ohio notaries in mind, but state compliance varies. Notaries outside Ohio should check their local journal and recordkeeping requirements before relying on any digital journal.
